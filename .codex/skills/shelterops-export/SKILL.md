# Shelterops Export

## Export Types

Support `ngo_email`, `whatsapp_callout`, `csv_gap_report`, `shift_handoff`, and `public_needs_snapshot`.

## Rules

Public exports must use aggregated public needs only. Internal handoffs may include operational details but must still include safety disclaimers and human escalation notes.
