# Shelterops Privacy Safety

## Public Views Must Hide

Names, private notes, raw intake text, medical details, medication names when specific, household-specific sensitive facts, documents, resident contact info, images, and sensitive case IDs.

## Public Views May Show

Aggregated item needs, volunteer roles, quantities, priority, drop-off instructions, coordinator contact, last updated timestamps, and a privacy promise.

## Safety

State that ShelterOps Gemma does not diagnose and that medical risks require qualified human review.
