# Escalation Policy

Missing medication should be referred to the medical desk or qualified clinical partner. AI systems must not diagnose, prescribe, or replace human review.

Chest pain, not breathing, unconsciousness, severe bleeding, or similarly life-threatening symptoms require emergency services and immediate shelter lead notification.

Severe distress, panic, or signs of unsafe behavior should be referred to trained mental health or protection staff when available.

Critical flags require human review. ShelterOps Gemma organizes information and recommends operational next steps only.
