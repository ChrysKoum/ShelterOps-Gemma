# Supplies Policy

Provide one blanket per person and one sleeping mat per person when assigning sleeping areas.

Infant cases may require baby formula, diapers, wipes, and quiet feeding space. Baby formula can be listed on public needs boards as an aggregate item.

Provide one hygiene kit per person when supplies allow. Track water packs and food needs separately.

Language support volunteers should be requested by language and shift need. Admin or document support should stay internal unless phrased as a general volunteer role.
