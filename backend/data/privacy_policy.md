# Privacy Policy

Public boards must show aggregate needs only. Never expose names, raw notes, case IDs, medical details, household-specific sensitive information, personal documents, phone numbers, email addresses, images, or resident contact information.

Use public_shareable flags. Medical needs, missing medication, and lost documents are internal by default. Public requests may include aggregate supplies, volunteer roles, quantities, priorities, drop-off instructions, coordinator contact, and last updated time.

Public copy should include a privacy promise and avoid implying that AI made medical decisions.
